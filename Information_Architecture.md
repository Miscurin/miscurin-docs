# Miscurin Information Architecture

Version: 1.0

Status: Active Development


# 1. Platform Overview

Miscurin consists of multiple connected systems.


## Customer Platform

Purpose:

Provide premium fashion shopping experience.


Components:

- Public Website
- Customer Account
- Checkout System
- Order Tracking


## Business Platform

Purpose:

Operate the company efficiently.


Components:

- Founder Operating System
- Staff Operating System
- Inventory Management
- Analytics
- Content Management

# 7. Permission Structure


Miscurin uses:


Department

↓

Role

↓

Permission

↓

Action


Example:


Product Assistant:

Can edit descriptions.


Product Manager:

Can approve product publishing.


Founder:

Full control.
